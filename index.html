About Us

Shri Yalamanchili Vasudevrao Kalyana Mantapam is a top notch convention hall built by Sindhanur Taluk Kammavari Sangam®. The Convention Hall offers various amenities that are ideal for all your special events. Our motto is customer satisfaction to the highest level. Our friendly staff extend their hospitality towards your guests and ensure their comfort at all times.

The Hall proffers posh interiors that will add glamour to your occasion. Moreover, the mantapam is experienced in both traditional and unconventional weddings, reception, pre and post-wedding ceremonies.

Perfectly located next to the national highway, finding the convention hall is effortless. Shri Yalamanchili Vasudevrao Kalyana Mantapam has a grand wedding hall that can host 3000+ guests at a time.The Mantapam is very near to environment and comforts our guests to maximum.

Fully air-conditioned halls and excellent amenities makes the convention hall a perfect choice for any type of event. Custom decorations as per the customers’ requirements are allowed.

The presence of an open stage in the Mantapam brings events close to the nature.Our Mantapam is furnished with a conference hall that makes it a perfect place for corporate events and seminars.

For detailed information on all the amenities please visit our amenities section.
